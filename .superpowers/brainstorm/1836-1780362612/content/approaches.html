C. Dual-Panel (Always-On Monitor)

Persistent monitoring strip across the top with live sparklines. Sidebar + category pages below. You always see system impact while toggling optimizations — ideal for before/after. No separate Dashboard page needed.

All approaches share

  • WinUI 3 NavigationView sidebar
  • Dark theme with Mica backdrop
  • Syncfusion charts for monitoring
  • Category pages with toggle controls
  • Profiles (presets + snapshots)

Key differences

  • A: Most room for monitoring, slight metric overlap
  • B: Everything on one page, risks long-scroll fatigue
  • C: Always-on monitoring, costs vertical space
