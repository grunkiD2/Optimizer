Key design decisions

P

Profiles: Two sections

Built-in Presets (grid cards): Light/Medium/Heavy/Cleanup with risk badge (Safe/Moderate/Aggressive), description, optimization count, and Apply button. Saved Snapshots (list): user-named with date, active count, Restore/Update/Delete actions. "Save Current State" button in header captures everything. Import/Export at the bottom for sharing.

H

History: Timeline + Impact

Impact chart at top: Syncfusion chart showing CPU/Memory/Health over time with vertical markers where optimizations were applied — visual proof that changes helped. Change log below: grouped by day, each entry shows optimization name, timestamp, category, reversibility badge, and per-item Undo button. One-time actions (like clearing temp files) show result ("Freed 2.1 GB") and are marked non-reversible.
