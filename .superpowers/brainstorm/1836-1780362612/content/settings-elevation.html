Non-elevated flow

App launches as asInvoker (MSIX compatible). Amber InfoBar persists at top of every page. Admin-required optimizations show 🛡️ shield and dimmed toggle. Clicking a shielded toggle → confirmation dialog → ElevationService.TryRelaunchElevated. After relaunch, green InfoBar confirms admin status and all toggles are enabled.
